# 📘 Chapter 9.3: Time Series Applications

---

## 🧠 What is Time Series in Real Life?

> Time series means **data collected over time**, where past values influence future values.

👉 You already learned how to build RNN for time series.
Now we will see **real-world applications**.

---

## 📈 Why Time Series is Important

Many real-world problems depend on:

* Past trends
* Patterns over time
* Seasonal changes

👉 RNN helps because it:

* Remembers past data
* Learns patterns
* Predicts future


---

* You look at past few days

## ⚠️ Challenges in Time Series

---

### ❌ Noise (random variation)

* Data not clean

---

### ❌ Sudden changes

* Unexpected events

---

### ❌ Long-term dependencies

* Hard for basic RNN

---

## 🚀 Better Approaches

For complex problems, use:

* LSTM
* GRU
* Transformer models

---

## 📌 Key Insight

> Time series applications rely heavily on patterns over time, and RNN is designed to capture those patterns.

## 📌 One-Line Summary
